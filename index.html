What is 'Engagement Rate Lift'?

"Lift" is a score that shows how much better (or worse) a hashtag performs compared to your account's average engagement rate. It's a performance score for your hashtags.

+10.4x Lift

Means posts with this tag performed **10.4 times better** than average. This is an excellent, high-impact hashtag.

-5.7x Lift

Means posts with this tag performed **worse** than average. This hashtag is likely hurting your post's reach.

Use Lift to find your winning tags and drop the ones that don't work.
